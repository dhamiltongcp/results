Who are the Looping Lappers?

2 years ago, a group of 10 brave souls agreed to test their will, strength and endurance in North America's largest 24 hour mountain bike race. In 2019, the team gathered with a few new faces to tackle the challenge again. The Looping Lappers team is for mountain bikers that are serious about having fun!
